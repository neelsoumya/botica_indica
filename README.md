# botica_indica

* A recipe inspired by Professor Shonku for those who want to:

   * eat a healthy and nutritious diet, and
   
   * do not have the time, and
   
   * are tired of eating Ramen or Maggi noodles


* Inspirations

    * https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Professor_Shonku#Inventions_of_Professor_Shonku

* Ingredients

   * Vegetables

      * Beans 
   
         * long beans
      
         * kidney beans
      
      * Garbanzo peas
   
      * Green lentils
   
      * Cabbage
      
      * Chopped Onions
   
   * Proteins
   
      * Eggs
   
      * Mushrooms
   
   * Spices
   
      * Indian spices
      
         * Turmeric
         
         * Garlic
         
         * Ginger
         
         * Chillies
   
* Protocol

  * Lots of patience
  
  * Slow cook in oil and spices
  
  * add chopped onions
  
  * add ingredients
  
  * taste periodically
  
   
* Additional ingredients

    * canned tuna (fried) 

    * nuts and seeds
    
    * yogurt
